Dynamic structure network 7 -- The growing process

2020-09-22

After baby training stop, our network will start waiting for the new dataset, then the growing training will start. Growing training is the main part of class increase process. Our growing process can base on the "activeness" of every node. So in this artical we will discuss about how backprop, forwardprop and node growing work.
To start this discussion, we have to introduce two concept: "Universal approximation theorem" and "No free lunch". The "universal approximation theorem" imply that neural networks can represent a wide variety of interesting functions when given appropriate weights. That's means whatever what kinds of function we try to learn, a huge network can certainly finish this job. But our algorithm still have possibility to fail. Becouse the optimize algorithm may not find the parameters. Also becouse the "no free lunch", there are no perfect algorithm.
